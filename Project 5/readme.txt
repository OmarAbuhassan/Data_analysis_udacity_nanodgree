Ford GoBike System Data Exploration 
By Omar Abuhassan

Dataset:
Ford GoBike System Dataset. 
This dataset includes information about individual rides made in a bike-sharing system covering the greater San Francisco Bay area.


Main findings:
- Users may have longer trip when they start between 11 AM and 4 PM. 
- Males are more likely to subscribe with our servecies than females.
- Female customers have the highest average time trip which is 1056 seconds and male subscribers have the lowest one which is 580 seconds. 
- Generally customers with age between 48 and 60 have the highest duration average.
- There is no big different between customers and subscribers in terms of start time.
